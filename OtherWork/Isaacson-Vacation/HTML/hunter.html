Hunter X Hunter

What Hunter X Hunter is like

Hunter X Hunter is A good show that shows us that anything is possible if we put our minds to it. It is center around two charactors who are best friends and do everything together. They train, hunt, and have fun together while being the best friends. There entire goal is to be strong enough to find one of there fathers.

Why I would like to go there

I would like to go to Hunter X Hunter so I can gain two more friends and be able to have fun in a nontradition area. Also it would be fun to be in a anime world where people can do anything.
